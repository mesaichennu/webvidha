01 Who We Are

Webvidha ("we", "our", or "us") is a web design and development agency based in Hyderabad, Telangana, India. We provide professional website creation services, helping businesses establish their online presence quickly and beautifully.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect personal information when you visit our website at webvidha.com, contact us, or use our services.

By using our website or engaging our services, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy. If you disagree, please refrain from using our website or services.

02 What We Collect

We collect two types of information — information you provide directly, and information collected automatically.

Information you provide:

  • Name and contact details (email address, phone number)
  • Business name and website requirements
  • Messages sent via our contact form or email
  • Payment and billing information (processed securely via third-party payment providers)
  • Any content, assets, or materials you share with us for project purposes

Information collected automatically:

Data Type Examples Purpose
Usage data Pages visited, time spent, clicks Improve website performance
Device data Browser type, OS, screen size Ensure compatibility
IP address Approximate geographic location Security and analytics
Referral data Source URL that brought you here Marketing analytics
Cookies Session tokens, preferences See Cookie Policy

We do not collect sensitive personal data such as government IDs, financial account numbers, health data, or biometric information.

03 How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to enquiries and provide our web design and development services
  • Process payments and manage project agreements
  • Send service updates, project status notifications, and invoices
  • Improve our website, services, and user experience
  • Analyse traffic and usage patterns through anonymised analytics
  • Comply with legal obligations and resolve disputes
  • Send marketing communications where you have given consent (you may opt out at any time)

We will never use your information for purposes incompatible with those stated above without notifying you first.

04 Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data on the following legal bases:

  • Contract performance — to fulfil our service agreements with you
  • Legitimate interests — to improve our services, prevent fraud, and ensure website security
  • Consent — for marketing communications and non-essential cookies (which you can withdraw at any time)
  • Legal obligation — to comply with applicable laws and regulatory requirements

05 Sharing Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share data with trusted third parties only where necessary:

  • Service providers — hosting platforms (Vercel), analytics (Google Analytics), payment processors, and email services that assist us in operating our business
  • Professional advisors — accountants or lawyers where legally required
  • Law enforcement — if required by court order, legal process, or to protect our rights and the safety of others
  • Business transfers — in the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of business assets, your data may be transferred as part of that transaction

All third-party service providers are contractually required to protect your data and use it only for the purposes we specify.

06 Data Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy:

  • Client project data — retained for the duration of the project plus 3 years for legal and warranty purposes
  • Contact enquiries — retained for 12 months from last contact
  • Financial records — retained for 7 years as required by Indian tax regulations
  • Analytics data — anonymised and retained for up to 26 months
  • Marketing data — retained until you unsubscribe or withdraw consent

When data is no longer required, it is securely deleted or anonymised.

08 Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorised access, loss, destruction, or alteration. These measures include:

  • HTTPS encryption on all web communications
  • Restricted access to personal data on a need-to-know basis
  • Secure storage with reputable cloud service providers
  • Regular review of data handling practices

While we take all reasonable precautions, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. In the event of a data breach affecting your rights, we will notify you promptly in accordance with applicable law.

09 Children's Privacy

Our website and services are intended for individuals aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 18.

If you believe a child has provided us with personal information without parental consent, please contact us immediately at hello@webvidha.com and we will promptly delete such information.

10 Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites, social media platforms, or partner services. These links are provided for your convenience and do not constitute an endorsement.

We have no control over the content, privacy practices, or policies of third-party websites. We encourage you to review the privacy policy of any site you visit through links on our website.

11 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or legal requirements. When we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

We encourage you to review this policy regularly. Your continued use of our website after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. For significant changes, we may also notify active clients directly by email.
